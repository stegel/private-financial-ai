How to Export from Your Brokerage

Fidelity ✓ Auto-detected

  1. Log in to Fidelity.com
  2. Transaction history: Accounts → Activity & Orders → select date range → Download (CSV)
  3. Positions: Accounts → Portfolio → Download (CSV)
  4. Upload both files here — they'll be detected automatically

Other Brokerages / Banks ✓ Generic CSV

  1. Export transactions as CSV from your brokerage or bank
  2. The file needs at minimum a Date and Amount column
  3. Optional: Description, Category columns are also used if present
